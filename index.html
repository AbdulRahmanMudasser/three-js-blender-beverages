About This Project

Pop & Roll is a vibrant brand that merges cutting-edge 3D design with striking visual storytelling. Specializing in dynamic 3D models and eye-catching packaging, the brand embodies creativity, modernity, and a playful energy. At its core, Pop & Roll aims to transform how audiences interact with digital and physical products through immersive visualization.

Technologies Used:

  • Three.js - WebGL Framework
  • Blender - 3D Modeling
  • CorelDRAW Graphic Suite - Wrapper Designing
  • GLTF format - 3D Asset Delivery
